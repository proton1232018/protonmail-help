The Ultimate Guide to Reaching ProtonMail Support

Secure. Private. Effective communication when it matters most.

Why You Might Need to Contact ProtonMail

  • Trouble logging in or password resets
  • Email delivery or encryption issues
  • Billing or subscription problems
  • Bridge integration issues
  • 2FA or recovery loss
  • Suspicious activity reports

Official Ways to Contact ProtonMail Support

  • Help Center: Best for quick answers
  • Email Support: Personalized help
  • Support Ticket: For paid users
  • Community Forum: Peer-to-peer help

Why No ProtonMail Contact Number?

To prevent impersonation and protect privacy, ProtonMail does not offer phone support. All support is done via secure written channels.

How to Contact ProtonMail Efficiently

  • Use official help or ticket channels
  • Include clear descriptions and screenshots
  • Provide account and device details
  • Be patient and follow up politely

Login Problems

Try password recovery via Help Center. If it fails, email support with recovery attempts and account details.

Billing and 2FA Issues

Describe your issue clearly in a ticket or email. Use backup codes for 2FA issues or contact support securely if lost.

Final Tips for Contacting ProtonMail

  • Use official channels only
  • Include your ProtonMail email, plan, and device info
  • Attach logs or screenshots for clarity
  • Be concise, polite, and patient
